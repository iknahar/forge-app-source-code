# Recording Backgrounds

Drop background images here (.jpg / .jpeg / .png / .heic). They ship inside
Forge.app and appear as selectable swatches in the recording editor's
Background panel (alongside Gradient, Solid color, and Hidden).

Users can also drop their own into:
  ~/Library/Application Support/Forge/Backgrounds/
or pick a custom image from the editor.

Recommended: 16:9-ish, ≥ 2560px wide, subtle (the recording sits on top).
